FAQ


General Questions

Q: What is this MEWFIT, and who is it for?

A: This app is a desktop fitness solution designed to help you track your workouts using your computer's built-in webcam for motion tracking. It also provides personalized fitness insights and diet recommendations tailored to your fitness goals. It's perfect for anyone looking to stay active and eat healthily, all from the comfort of their home.

Q: Do I need special equipment to use this app?

A: No special equipment is required. The app works with your computer’s built-in webcam for motion tracking. However, if you wish to enhance your workouts, you can use exercise accessories like mats, resistance bands, or weights.

Q: Can I use the app without an internet connection?

A: Some features, like motion tracking and offline workout routines, work without an internet connection. However, features like meal recommendations and updates require internet access.


Motion Capture and Tracking

Q: How does the motion tracking feature work?

A: The motion tracking feature uses your computer’s webcam to detect and analyze your movements during workouts. It helps ensure proper form and provides real-time feedback to improve your exercise technique.

Q: Is the motion capture feature accurate?

A: The motion tracking system is powered by advanced algorithms designed to provide accurate movement analysis. While it performs well in most settings, ensuring proper lighting and a clear background can enhance accuracy.

Q: Is the webcam feed stored or shared?

A: No, your privacy is our priority. The webcam feed is processed locally on your computer and is not stored, shared, or uploaded to any servers.

Q: Can I use an external webcam for better motion tracking?

A: Yes, the app supports both built-in and external webcams. Using a high-quality external webcam may improve tracking accuracy.


Diet and Meal Recommendations

Q: How are diet recommendations personalized?

A: The app uses the information you provide—such as age, weight, fitness goals, and dietary preferences—to create a tailored meal plan. It also adjusts recommendations based on your activity levels and progress.

Q: Can I log my own meals instead of following the app’s recommendations?

A: Yes, the app allows you to log your meals manually. This feature helps you track your caloric intake and compare it with your daily goals.

Q: Does the app accommodate dietary restrictions?

A: Absolutely! The app supports various dietary preferences and restrictions, including vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian and more. You can specify your preferences during the setup process.

Q: What are the system requirements for using this app?

A: The app requires a computer running Windows 10 or later / macOS 10.14 or later, a built-in or connected webcam, and a stable internet connection for updates and diet recommendations.

Q: My webcam isn’t working. What should I do?

A: Ensure your webcam is enabled and not being used by another application. You can also check your computer’s settings to verify that the app has permission to access the webcam.

Q: Can I use this app on multiple devices?

A: Yes, you can install the app on multiple computers, but your data will sync only if you log in using the same account.


Privacy and Data

Q: What data does the app collect?

A: The app collects basic fitness and dietary information you provide, such as age, weight, and activity level. Your webcam feed is processed locally and is not stored or shared.

Q: How do I delete my account and data?

A: You can delete your account and all associated data by visiting the settings page and following the account deletion process.


Customisation and User Experience

Q: Can I change the app’s interface or theme?

A: Yes, the app offers Dark Mode to accomodate to the your user preferences.
